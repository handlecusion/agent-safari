# Roadmap

The maintained roadmap lives in `docs/ROADMAP.md`:

- P0: reliability and CLI/MCP parity.
- P1: screenshot and network-capture hardening.
- P2: browser/session model, tabs, profiles, and artifact lifecycle.

Passkey/WebAuthn automation is explicitly out of scope for the current roadmap.

For release readiness, see `docs/RELEASE_CHECKLIST.md`.
